Shrek!!!

Shrek's, an ogre, peaceful life is disrupted when a group of fairy tale creatures, banished from the kingdom of Duloc by Lord Farquaad, invade his swamp. In order to get his home back, Shrek agrees to rescue Princess Fiona from a distant tower as per agreed with Farquaad, who wants to marry her to become king. Along the way, Shrek is joined by a talking donkey. He eventually falls in love with Fiona, only to discover that she has a secret curse that turns her into an ogre every night. The story culminates with Shrek confessing his love, Fiona remaining in her ogre form forever, and the two living happily ever after.
