Me, Rhythm, and Blues.

This music genre has been a childhood memory, is a present experience, and possibly my future.
Never had I ever gone through a week without music, and majority of which is Beyonce, and Sza.

What makes it so fun to listen to?

  • Groove - the music's flow gives you a steady energy. It isn't too shallow, nor is it too much to bear; just right.
  • Vibe - Similar to groove, Vibe is the general energy from the music. it could be hues of sad feelings (rhythm and blues) or mellow, calming and happy music.
  • Lyrics - The lyrics matter. Even if most R&B I listen to contain mature content, it's the art of seeing over the lyrics that make R&B so fun.
