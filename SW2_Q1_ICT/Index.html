Our Vision

We aim to unite all the curly-headed broccoli heads, athletes, students, and the youth to make a community beneficial to us. we want to create a small world where everyone, and everything is curly. Frathouses have built-in salons, and departments for everyone to be included. No curly-boy is ever going to feel alone i ther hair struggles, no one will be abandoned in the banishments of frizz and hair damage, no one will have to suffer being forced to wash your hair 3 days sooner your washday. One day, every kulot won't have to fight for expensive hair styling prices.
